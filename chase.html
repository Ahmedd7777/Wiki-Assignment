Pokey Pummel

Pokey Pummel is a 4-player minigame from the mario party series. Originally found in Mario Party 7 and brought back in Mario Party: The Top 100, and Mario Party Superstars

Introduction

The players stand in the desert as Pokeys rise from the ground above them. The players are then given large wooden mallets. A timer counts down from 3 before the game begins.

Gameplay

The goal for each player is to use their hammer to destroy segments of the Pokey before the other players. Each segment of the Pokey takes 5 hits to break except for Mario Party: The Top 100, it takes 3 hits. The first player to whack away the Pokeys head wins.

If no one is able to completely knock down the Pokey in 5 minutes in Mario Party 7 or in 3 minutes in Mario Party Superstars, the minigame will end in a tie

Ending

In Mario Party 7 the rest of the Pokeys go back in the ground when the first player wins, and the game ends. The winning players character celebrates, while the losers characters wallow. In all other installments, the game ends when the first 3 players finish, this cuts off player 4 from finishing.

Controls

Every version requires the player to consecutively press the A button on the given console's controller.
